Secure Login

Role-based access for Admins and Students. Each user sees only what they need.

Credit Issuance

Admins can issue credits to students. Each issuance is recorded on the Ethereum blockchain.

// How It Works
Simple 4-Step Process
01

Register Student

Admin registers students in the system with their department and details.

02

Issue Credits

Admin issues course credits and grades after evaluations are complete.

03

Record on Blockchain

The credit record is pushed to the smart contract on Ethereum Sepolia Testnet.

04

Student Verifies

Students login, view their results and click to verify the transaction hash on Etherscan.
